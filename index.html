About Me

I’m a sophomore at the University of Chicago, where I’m pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Media Arts and Design. My passion lies at the intersection of technology and creativity, where I strive to solve complex problems using innovative programming algorithms. I am proficient in Python and Java, with extensive experience in developing software solutions that address real-world challenges. My love for digital art complements my technical skills, allowing me to blend artistic vision with computational prowess. This unique combination fuels my enthusiasm for building cutting-edge AR/VR technology solutions. In addition to my programming expertise, I am skilled in graphics animation, 3D design, and sculpting. These skills enable me to create immersive and visually stunning digital experiences. My goal is to continue exploring the synergy between art and technology, pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in the digital realm. Thank you for visiting my website. I look forward to sharing my journey and projects with you!
